Tickets Report

There are two types of reports in Searbia Railway: montly and ride reports. You can select one of these two by clicking on buttons.

Monthly Tickets

This report shows how many tickets were sold on the selected month. Month is chosen by selecting month from the combobox and selecting the year.

When you click on the "Show tickets" button, there will be list of all sold tickets for the selected month.

Tickets for the ride

You have to choose line and date for the desired ride. Then, by clicking the "Show tickets" button, list of all sold tickets will be shown.
