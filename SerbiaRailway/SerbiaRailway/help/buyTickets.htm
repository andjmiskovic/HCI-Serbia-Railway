How to Buy/Reserve a Ticket

First, you should choose the wagon by selecting the wagon number.

When you select the wagon, your next stop is selecting the seat. Every row has 4 seats and every seat can be FIRST or SECOND class. For first class seats, you have to pay some extra money. You choose the seat by clicking on seat number.

At the bottom of the page, you have all the data about your desired ride. All you have to do now is to choose if you want to RESERVE or BUY this ticket. You can find all your tickets on the "My tickets" page.
