Searching timetable

First, you need to choose starting point of your journey. You have a list of all available stations in our system. Select one of those. Then, you do this for the ending point also.

There is a calendar on the left. You have to choose the date of your journey by selecting the number on the calendar.

When you have chosen everything, please click SEARCH button. Page will fill with all the options for your travel. Ride option look like this:

You can see departure and arrival time, the price and the train manufacturer. Also, when you click on "View on map", this window will show up:

By clicking on the "Reserve/Buy" button, you will open new window and you will be able to select the seat for your ride. Goodluck!
