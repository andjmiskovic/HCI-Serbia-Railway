Timetable

By choosing your starting and ending point along with the date of your travel, you will be able to reserve and buy tickets.

You can select any station from the list of available stations we offer. You cannot search tickets for past dates. By clicking on the "Search" button, you will be able to see details about timeline (time, price, train manufacturer...). When you click on "View on Map" button, you can see map view of stations.
